# 6-Month Plan: Austin AV Tech to Lease-Ready Asset

## Goal
Build a top local-intent SEO property and lease lead flow to an Austin AV company.

## Month 1-2: Foundation
- Publish 12 high-intent pages/posts
- Add service-area and use-case pages
- Implement lead capture form + CTA blocks

## Month 3-4: Authority + Leads
- Publish comparison and buyer-intent pages
- Build citation + local trust signals
- Start tracking lead volume and intent quality

## Month 5-6: Leasing Readiness
- Build partner pitch deck + lead quality report
- Test outreach to 15 local AV businesses
- Target first monthly leasing agreement

## KPI targets
- 20+ indexed pages by month 3
- 30 qualified leads by month 6
- 1 leasing deal by month 6
