## Quick answer
Great projector results depend on throw distance, room light control, screen size, and mount placement.

## Checklist
- Confirm room dimensions and throw range
- Pick screen size by seating distance
- Choose ceiling/wall mount position
- Plan cable routing and power
- Test image geometry and focus
