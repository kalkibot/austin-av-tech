## Austin AV Buyer Checklist

Use this before selecting any AV vendor.

- Define room use-cases and business outcomes
- Set budget bands (hardware, install, support)
- Confirm acoustic and network requirements
- Require scope document with acceptance criteria
- Confirm support SLA and escalation path
