## Quick answer
For reliable home coverage, map entry points first, then choose Wi-Fi or PoE based on stability and scale.

## Checklist
- Define coverage zones (door, driveway, backyard)
- Choose Wi-Fi vs PoE architecture
- Confirm recording retention settings
- Validate night visibility and motion alerts
